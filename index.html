Take your website security seriously

Your sites safety and security should be top priority. Safeguarding your WordPress site from malware, plugin vulnerabilities or potentially weak passwords shouldn't be complicated or scary.

Sucuri

Free site check scan can quickly check your site for infections. Sucuri's top rated service will remove, prevent and monitor your site for malware.

iThemes Security

Bundled with tutorials, a Pro and a Free version with over 2K five star ratings on wordpress.org. That protects your website with over 30 ways.

Security Ninja

Provides you with detail help for any modifications needed to make your site more secure from any vulnerabilities found after over 30 security tests.

Wordfence

100% free and best of all Multi-Site compatible. Offers scanning and Real-time blocking capabilities of malicious attacks to your website.

Your Website Security Public Service Announcement

A friendly reminder the security plugins mentioned above, are based of on our usage and recommendation.

Most people take action to up their website security after the have fallen victim to a breach. Installing any of the plugins mention above will not make your website unhackable, they are meant to protect and make your website less vulnerable.

Our recommendation is install a plugin if needed that meets your needs and is understandably easy to use by you.
